About

About

EcoSteps: Running Towards Change is more than just a fun run—it’s a movement that combines fitness with environmental responsibility. This event aims to inspire individuals, families, and communities to take active steps toward sustainability while promoting a healthy lifestyle. Through this run, we encourage eco-friendly habits such as reducing plastic waste, using sustainable materials, and supporting green initiatives.


Beyond race day, EcoSteps advocates for active mobility—encouraging participants to walk or run for short-distance trips instead of relying on vehicles. By choosing to walk when possible, we can reduce car dependency, ease traffic congestion, and lower carbon emissions, all while improving personal health. Whether you’re a seasoned runner or a first-time participant, EcoSteps offers an opportunity to make a difference—one step at a time.


HOW THE FUN RUN SUPPORTS SUSTAINABILITY

Reusable Water Stations

Instead of single-use plastic bottles, runners are encouraged to bring reusable water bottles, with refill stations available at checkpoints.

Eco-friendly Race Kits

Participants receive race kits that include biodegradable or reusable materials, such as cloth race bibs and sustainable giveaways.

Tree-planting Initiative

For every runner who participates, a tree will be planted in designated areas within Gubat to help restore green spaces.

Waste Management

Proper waste segregation bins will be placed along the route to encourage responsible disposal of trash.

Partnership with Local Businesses

Collaborations with vendors who use eco-friendly packaging and locally sourced products.

Fun Run Route


The event kicks off at the front of Gubat Municipal Park, a vibrant community hub that showcases the town's commitment to providing recreational spaces for its residents and visitors.

  • From Gubat Municipal Park, runners will proceed along Diversion Road, heading towards the Gubat Fire Station, where the first checkpoint will be located.

  • The course will continue towards the end of Diversion Road, offering participants a view of the serene surroundings. As runners make their way to the second checkpoint, a Water Station will be available midway through the diversion, providing hydration to ensure participants stay refreshed throughout the race.

  • After reaching the end of Diversion Road, runners will head towards El Mabuhay Gas Station, which serves as the second checkpoint. From there, runners will continue onto Junction Abuyog - Gubat - Ariman Road, making their way back towards town.

  • The course then leads through the bustling streets of Gubat Public Market, a central landmark known for its vibrant atmosphere and local culture.

  • Finally, the route loops back to Gubat Municipal Park, where the finish line awaits amidst cheering crowds and celebration.

Event FAQs

Event FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions about EcoSteps

What is EcoSteps: Running Towards Change?

EcoSteps is a sustainability-focused fun run that promotes fitness while advocating for environmental responsibility through eco-friendly initiatives.

Who can join the event?

Anyone can join! The event is open to all ages and fitness levels, from beginners to experienced runners.

When and where will the event take place?

The event will take place at Gubat Municipal Park on August 30th, starting at 5:00 AM. Participants are encouraged to arrive early to check in and warm up before the run begins.

How do I register for the event?

You can register online through our official website or visit our designated registration booths.

What should I bring on race day?

Bring your race bib, a reusable water bottle, and wear comfortable running gear.
